    • Procurement

      Thanks to the participation of citizens and companies, we gather PET plastic from selective collection.
    • Extrusion

      From the collection bin to our extrusion department, the resource is integrated to be decontaminated and transformed into food grade plastic sheeting.
    • Production

      The packaging is manufactured from 100% recycled RPET material in our automated and sterile facilities.
    • Use

      Ecofriendly packaging is put back on the shelves of retailers and food manufacturers.
    • Recycling

      Packaging is placed in the household recycling bin and redirected to sorting centers.
    • Reuse

      The sorted, cleaned and crushed material is ready for a new life.
